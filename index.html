About Us

Our company was started in 1965 by our late father Mr. P. Dayanandam and he specialized in tricolour, cut-colour, line, foil printing and embossing blocks. Our main motto is to deliver quality products on time which we believe is the key reason for triumphantly running the company for more than half a century.

Services

Line Blocks

These are made of zinc metal and are used for Gold foil printing in wedding invitations, packaging industies and many more.

Emboss Blocks

These are produced in zinc metal and are used as female part in embossing.

Polymer Blocks

These are manufactured in rubber and are used as male part of emboss blocks.

Name Plates

These are made in brass and steel metals and are used for a variety of display purposes.
